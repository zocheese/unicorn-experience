About This Module

Geriatrics 101

Description:

Welcome to the eLearning module Caring for Older Persons.

This module is to assist clinicians to prepare for a change in patient demographics. We canexpect to treat more older people at our health services in the future due to our ageingpopulation. The content touches on age discrimination,function, frailty, taking time to adequatelyaccess and listen to patients and family concerns. The advantages of using Multidisciplinaryresources to help patients to get well and to go on to live a life they love. There are two activitiesfor participants to test themselves during the learning.

Objective:

After completing this module, you will be able to:

  • Challenge common misconceptions about ageing to ensure holistic and person centredcare.
  • Identify health issues accurately in older persons without mistakenly attributing them totheir age.
  • Identify ways to collaborate with the multidisciplinary team to provide integrated care forthe older person.
  • Utilise appropriate resources that aim to increase older person's confidence in theirabilities and independence
